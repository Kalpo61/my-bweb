guardian | Bromance

Guardian is a 2018 chinese web series starring Bai Yu and Zhu Yilong. An adaptation of the 2012 fantasy-denmei web novel of the same name by Priest, the series was released on the video platform Touku between june 13 and july 25, 2018. The series follows Zhao Yunlan(Bai) and Shen Wei(Zhu), a detective and a professor who investigate supernatural phenomena. Though the novel depicts their relationship as romantic in nature, the web series depicts the relationship as a platonic friendship with homoerotic subtext in response to chinese prohibiting depictions of same-sex relationships on television.

The series ws widly popular on web and received a total of 1.8 billion views by August 2018. That same month, Guardian was pulled from Youku amid a crackdown on "harmful and vulgar" internet content but was later re-uploaded
